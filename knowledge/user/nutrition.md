# User Nutrition Plan

## Goal: Lean Bulk

Increase body weight from 63.4 kg to 72-75 kg while minimising fat gain.

## Daily Targets

| Nutrient | Target | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Calories | 2800 kcal (start) | Adjust ±100 based on rate of gain |
| Protein | 140-170g | 2.2-2.7 g/kg bodyweight |
| Carbohydrates | 300-400g | Fill remaining after protein/fat |
| Fat | 60-80g | Minimum 60g for hormone health |
| Fiber | 30g+ | Gut health, satiety |
| Water | 3L+ | Hydration for performance |

## Meal Timing (Flexible)

| Meal | Timing | Contents |
|------|--------|----------|
| Pre-workout | 1-2 hours before | 40-60g carbs + 20g protein |
| Post-workout | Within 2 hours | 40-60g carbs + 30-40g protein |
| Breakfast | Wake-up | Protein + carbs |
| Lunch | Midday | Protein + carbs + vegetables |
| Dinner | Evening | Protein + carbs + vegetables |
| Snacks | As needed | Protein shake, fruit, nuts |

## Tracking Method

- **Cronometer** for all food logging
- **GymOS** imports CSV from Cronometer to display macro progress
- Weekly weight check to adjust calories
- Protein priority: minimum 140g every day

## Rate of Weight Gain

- Target: 0.25-0.5 kg per week
- If gaining <0.25 kg/week for 2 weeks: +100 kcal/day
- If gaining >0.5 kg/week: -100 kcal/day
- Adjustments made in carbs first (increase/decrease)

## Supplement Stack

- Whey protein (as needed to hit protein target)
- Creatine monohydrate (5g daily)
- Vitamin D3 + K2 (daily)
- Magnesium (evening, for sleep)

## Key Principles

- Protein is the #1 priority — never skip it
- Carbs fuel training — don't fear them on a bulk
- Fats are essential for hormones — never go below 60g
- Hydration directly impacts performance
- Consistency beats perfection — 90% adherence is excellent

# User Goal

## Primary Goal

Build the best aesthetic physique possible through data-driven hypertrophy training.

## Specific Objectives

| Objective | Target | Timeline |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Body weight | 72–75 kg | 6–12 months |
| Shoulder width | Increased lateral delt size | Ongoing |
| Upper chest development | Clear upper chest definition | Ongoing |
| Back width (V-taper) | Wider lats, visible from front | Ongoing |
| Arm size | Proportionate, 40+ cm flexed | Ongoing |

## Secondary Objectives

- Strength progression on all major lifts (indicator of muscle growth)
- Consistency: 85%+ of scheduled sessions completed
- Nutrition compliance: daily protein ≥140g, calories ≥2800
- Body composition: visible muscle gain with minimal fat gain
- Maintenance of lower body strength (not a focus, but not neglected)

## Lifestyle Constraints

- Train in a commercial gym with standard equipment
- Full-time work — training is scheduled around work hours
- No dietary restrictions (eats everything)
- Prefers morning/evening workouts depending on schedule  

## Motivation

- Aesthetic physique — wants to look muscular and athletic
- Not interested in powerlifting numbers
- Wants sustainable habits, not crash diets or extreme programs
- Values evidence-based training over bro-science

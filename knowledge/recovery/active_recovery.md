# Active Recovery

## What is Active Recovery

Low-intensity activity between training sessions to promote blood flow, reduce soreness, and accelerate recovery — without adding fatigue.

## Guidelines

| Aspect | Recommendation |
|--------|---------------|
| Perceived exertion | 2–4/10 |
| Heart rate | <120 bpm |
| Duration | 20–40 min |
| Timing | Day after hard session or on rest days |

## Protocols

### Light Cardio
- Walking: 20–40 min outdoors
- Cycling: Very easy pace, low resistance
- Swimming: Easy, non-maximal
- Rowing: Light, technique-focused

### Mobility Work
- Full body foam rolling (10 min)
- Dynamic stretching (10 min)
- Yoga flow (15–30 min)

### What NOT to Do
- No high intensity
- No heavy resistance training
- No competition or max effort
- No new exercises (technique practice is fine)

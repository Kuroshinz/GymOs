# Macronutrient Guidelines

## Protein

| Goal | g/kg bodyweight | Sources |
|------|----------------|---------|
| Sedentary | 0.8 | General population |
| Active (recreational) | 1.2–1.6 | Most gym-goers |
| Muscle gain (bulking) | 1.6–2.2 | Optimal for hypertrophy |
| Cutting (deficit) | 2.0–2.4 | Preserve muscle in deficit |

**Per meal**: 20–40g, spaced every 3–4 hours.
**Sources**: Chicken, beef, fish, eggs, whey, soy, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese.

## Carbohydrates

| Activity level | g/kg bodyweight | Notes |
|---------------|----------------|-------|
| Low activity | 2–3 | Desk job, minimal cardio |
| Moderate | 3–5 | Regular training 3–5x/week |
| High | 5–8 | Athletes, daily training |
| Very high | 8–12 | Endurance athletes |

**Timing**: Prioritize carbs around workouts.
**Sources**: Rice, oats, potatoes, sweet potatoes, quinoa, pasta, fruit.

## Fat

| Goal | % total calories | g/kg |
|------|-----------------|------|
| Minimum health | 20–25% | 0.5–0.8 |
| Moderate | 25–30% | 0.8–1.0 |
| Ketogenic | 60–75% | 2.0+ |

**Sources**: Olive oil, avocado, nuts, seeds, fatty fish, egg yolks.
**Essential fats**: Omega-3 (EPA/DHA) 1–3g/day from fish or supplement.

## Caloric Recommendations

| Goal | Surplus/Deficit | Rate |
|------|----------------|------|
| Muscle gain | +300–500 kcal/day | ~0.5kg/month |
| Fat loss | -300–500 kcal/day | ~0.5kg/week |
| Maintenance | TDEE | Stable weight |

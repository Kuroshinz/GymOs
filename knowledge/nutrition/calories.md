# Calorie Guidelines

## Energy Balance

| State | Calories vs TDEE | Result |
|-------|-----------------|--------|
| Surplus | Above TDEE | Weight gain (muscle + some fat) |
| Maintenance | At TDEE | Weight stable |
| Deficit | Below TDEE | Weight loss (fat + some muscle) |

## Current User Targets

## Adjustment Rules

| Condition | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Gaining 0.25-0.5 kg/week | Keep current calories |
| Gaining <0.25 kg/week for 2 weeks | +100 kcal/day |
| Gaining >0.5 kg/week | -100 kcal/day |
| Weight stable for 2+ weeks on surplus | +200 kcal/day |

| Macronutrient | Calories/g | Target g | Calories | % of Total |
|--------------|-----------|----------|----------|------------|
| Protein | 4 | 140-170 | 560-680 | 20-24% |
| Carbohydrates | 4 | 300-400 | 1200-1600 | 43-57% |
| Fat | 9 | 60-80 | 540-720 | 19-26% |

## Key Points

- Consistency over perfection — hitting targets 80% of days is excellent
- Weigh yourself daily, same time (morning, after bathroom, before food)
- Use weekly trend, not daily fluctuations, to adjust
- Rate of gain should never exceed 0.5 kg/week (excess fat gain)
- Bulking too fast = more fat than muscle; slow and steady wins

# Protein for Muscle Gain

## Key Findings

### Total Daily Protein
- 1.6–2.2 g/kg/day is optimal for muscle gain
- Beyond 2.2 g/kg shows diminishing returns
- 2.0–2.4 g/kg/day during caloric deficit helps preserve muscle

### Per Meal
- 20–40g per meal is sufficient
- Beyond 40g per meal: oxidation increases significantly
- 3–4 meals per day spread evenly optimizes MPS

### Timing
- Protein timing within a 4–6 hour window around training is sufficient
- The "anabolic window" is wider than traditionally thought
- Pre-sleep protein (casein) can benefit overnight MPS

### Protein Sources
- Animal-based: Complete amino acid profile, higher leucine
- Plant-based: Lower leucine, need ~20–30% more total
- Whey: Fast absorption, highest leucine
- Casein: Slow absorption, good pre-bed
